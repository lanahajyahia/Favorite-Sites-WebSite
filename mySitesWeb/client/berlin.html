M&M'S Berlin is located on Kurfürstendamm, the city's most popular shopping street, the doors to a chocolate wonderland of over 3,000 square meters. Unique design elements are dedicated to Berlin's artistic roots, complemented by a private roof terrace with panoramic views.

The M&M’S Café is the first of its kind. Open every day during regular store hours, guests will be immersed into a visual & flavor experience. M&M’S Ice Cream Dips are an ice cream treat shaped like our M&M’S candies that is dipped into a colorful coating that freezes instantly, creating an M&M’S ice cream treat. M&M’S Mix-Ins offer guests a choice of five different delicious flavors of our M&M’S candies mixed for a frozen delight. Also offered are fresh baked sugar cookies made with M&M’S candies baked into every bite. The Café also has available an assortment of local hot and cold beverages.
